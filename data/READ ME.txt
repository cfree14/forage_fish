

KEY TO DATA FILES

1. **all_stocks** - meta-data on all RAM stocks

2. **potential_fish_predator_prey_data** - identify candidate fish prey/predator data from the RAM Legacy Database

3. **prey_data** - trimmed/formatted prey data

4. **fish_predator_data** - trimmed/formatted fish predator data

5. **nonfish_predator_data** - trimmed/formatted non-fish predator data

6. **fish_pred_data_final** - linked fish predator and prey data (final)

7. **nonfish_pred_data_final** - linked non-fish predator and prey data (final)8. **data_final** - merged linked fish and non-fish files
